What is Ceroc?

Ceroc is an alternative spin on modern jive style dancing. What makes this style of dancing so versatile is that it’s easy to learn,
social and you can dance it pretty much any song, whether it’s a favorite pop song all the way through to dubstep.

Is Ceroc for me?

Ceroc is for any one, at any age and any fitness level. The longer you commit, the cooler the moves. At bare minimum,
you have 3 hours worth of social dancing each night for $10. You may choose to go through the levels, or just have fun!
